# Scope

## Included in the initial restoration

- exact-version identification;
- official and essential community bug fixes;
- Windows 11 compatibility;
- modern resolution and display profiles;
- safe installation, backup and restoration;
- modern camera and time controls;
- map and map-mode visual restoration;
- minimap and map-control restoration;
- readable windows, typography, icons and button states;
- improved campaign and character selection;
- progressive tutorial and quick-reference material;
- accessibility-oriented audio improvements;
- original project tooling and documentation.

## Explicitly excluded from the initial phase

- total conversions;
- alternate universes;
- additional historical start dates;
- large content expansions;
- new core mechanics;
- major rebalance projects;
- mandatory integration of Vanilla+ packages such as DVIP or TASS.

Vanilla+ projects may still be studied as technical references or as sources of isolated, clearly understood fixes.

## Compatibility target

Primary:

- Steam edition of Crusader Kings Complete;
- Windows 11;
- 16:9 displays at 1080p, 1440p and 4K;
- dual-monitor configurations.

Secondary targets will only be added after the primary baseline is stable.
